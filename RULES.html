Welcome to the Vibe Check

Welcome to FuzzyBuzzy, where the chat is fast and the vibes are fire. To keep the community a safe space and fun—on god—you gotta follow these rules.

This is an interactive guide to our community rules. Use the navigation on the left to explore, or just click the topics below to see what's good. Read 'em, internalize 'em, and let's send it.

How this works

Click any navigation item on the left to jump to a section. Click any of the rule headers in a section to expand it and read the details. Easy, fr fr.

Keep the Vibe Positive

This section is all about how we treat each other. To keep things chill, everyone needs to bring main character energy. Click each rule to expand and see the details.

Treat everyone with main character energy—respectful, period. If you're sending shade, harassment, bullying, or hate (based on race, gender, etc.), you're giving villain arc. Don't be cheugy; be kind.

Do not be a troll or start unnecessary beef (flaming). If you have to disagree, make it a chill conversation, not a 3 AM Twitter war. Keep it friendly, or take it to the DMs (that’s called discretion).

Don't share anyone's personal info (pics, phone numbers, location). Doxxing is foul and a straight-up L—it will get you gone. Keep things on the down low.

Hard No's (Prohibited Content)

These are the big ones. If you post this, you're the one catching the consequences. Fr fr. Click each rule to see what's up.

Don't post anything that's illegal, ever. No links for scams, hacks, or other majorly sus activity. We don't need that kind of energy, touch grass instead.

If content promotes violence, hatred, or discrimination, it’s a hard delete. We don't do that here.

Do not flood the chat or use bots to ruin the feed. If you're sending malware or phishing links, you're getting banned—no warning. That’s just basic.

No explicit material in public rooms. Protected rooms are on you, but local laws are still the boss. Don't mess up the bag.

Room Check

Here's the tea on chat rooms, public and private. Keep it clean. Click below for the rules.

Keep room names clean and relevant. Don't be messy with the titles; we're trying to keep things organized. If the title is yikes, it gets changed.

If you lock a room, you're the leader. Make sure your crew is following the main rules (Sections 1 and 2), even behind the password. Be responsible for your squad.

The Mods Are Watching

This part is about reporting, consequences, and how we keep the vibe safe. Expand the rules to get the info.

If you see something sus, send me proof (report it immediately). Do not try to handle it yourself and start drama. Let the pros deal with the cringe.

Admins and Mods have the final say (period). If you break the rules, you might get:

  1. A formal warning (the first strike).
  2. A temporary ban/kick (time out).
  3. A permanent RIP (account termination).

Sometimes a rule isn't *technically* broken, but the vibe is ruined. If it harms the community, we'll step in to keep the chat quality high. We're prioritizing the glow-up.
